## Chief Data Officers Council

The Chief Data Officers Council (CDOC) was established by the [Foundations for Evidence-Based Policymaking Act of 2018](https://www.congress.gov/115/plaws/publ435/PLAW-115publ435.pdf) (Evidence Act). The Council is comprised of Chief Data Officers (CDOs) from CFO Act agencies and other designated federal agencies. It serves as a forum to:

- Advance the use of data to support mission delivery and evidence-based decision-making across the federal government
- Share best practices and promote data governance
- Coordinate and collaborate on federal data policy, management, and use
- Support the implementation of the Evidence Act and related OMB guidance
